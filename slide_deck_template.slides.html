US Flights 2018 Dataset¶

by Islam M. Saad¶

Investigation Overview¶

  • What's the percentage of Arrival/Departure delays?
  • What are US Operating Carriers Airlines that are having many flights at 2018?
  • What's the direct relationship between Distance and Arrival/Departure delays?
  • What are the preferred months for flights to occur?
  • Is Arrival Delay & Departure Delay increasing with long distant trips?

Dataset Overview¶

  • US Flights 2018 Dataset contains about 7.2 million observations and 27 variables.
  • There are 16 Airlines operating Carriers, which facilitate US flights in 2018.
  • We use a random sample of 10% of Dataset to speed up processing time and sample express the total population as well.
  • We have 11627 cancelled flights from 10% sample of 7.2Million Records and then we will drop it as we only study delay of flights.

What is the structure of your dataset?¶

7.2 million records dataset and we will use random sample of 10% (about 720K) records in order to ease and agile the analysis.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

Arrival Delay, Departure Delay

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

OP_CARRIER, Actual Elapsed Time, ORIGIN Airports, DEST Airports, Distance, DATE, Arrival Time, Departure Time.

Departure Delay & Arrival Delay¶

  • We can conclude that we have 239,738 delayed flights due to Arrival delay which is 35.63% of total flights in our sample.
  • We can conclude that we have 232,385 delayed flights due to Departure delay which is 34.54% of total flights in our sample
  • We found that number of flights Delayed in Arrival differs from number of flights Delayed in Departure

xbin_edges = np.arange(-117, flights['ARR_DELAY'].max()+150, 150)
ybin_edges = np.arange(-109,    flights['DEP_DELAY'].max()+120, 120)

# count number of points in each bin
xbin_idxs = pd.cut(flights['ARR_DELAY'], xbin_edges, right = False,
                    include_lowest = True, labels = False).astype(int)
ybin_idxs = pd.cut(flights['DEP_DELAY'], ybin_edges, right = False,
                    include_lowest = True, labels = False).astype(int)

pts_per_bin = flights.groupby([xbin_idxs, ybin_idxs]).size()
pts_per_bin = pts_per_bin.reset_index()
pts_per_bin = pts_per_bin.pivot(index = 'ARR_DELAY', columns = 'DEP_DELAY').values

z_wts = flights['DISTANCE'] / pts_per_bin[xbin_idxs, ybin_idxs]

# plot the data using the calculated weights
plt.hist2d(data = flights, x = 'ARR_DELAY', y = 'DEP_DELAY', weights = z_wts,
           bins = [xbin_edges, ybin_edges], cmap = 'viridis_r', cmin = 0.5);
plt.title("Arrival Delay & Departure Delay & Flight Distance Relationship")
plt.xlabel('ARR_DELAY in minutes')
plt.ylabel('DEP_DELAY in minutes');
plt.colorbar(label = 'mean(DISTANCE) in Miles');
